# How to Buy/Sell/Trade ANKR

**ANKR** Tokens can be bought and traded at most popular crypto exchanges. As an ERC-20 token,  ANKR can be stored on any ERC20 compatible wallet, Centralized Exchange, or DEX.

{% hint style="success" %}
